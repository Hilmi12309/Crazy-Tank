About Us

Why Choose us?

Welcome to Crazy Tanks, where the rumble of an engine and the gleam of cold steel speak a language we've been fluent in for over 60 years. We're not just a dealership; we're a legacy built on the unwavering belief that some challenges demand more than just a truck â€“ they demand a tank.

Born from the inventive spirit of "Crazy" Hamburger, a former armored cavalry sergeant with a knack for repurposing the un-repurposable, Crazy Tanks started in a dusty lot outside of Barstow, California, offering militarized surplus to farmers battling particularly stubborn boulders and ambitious off-grid homesteaders. Today, we're proud to be the premier purveyor of tracked vehicles for discerning individuals and organizations worldwide.

Our mission is simple: to connect you with the perfect armored solution for your needs, whether it's navigating the most unforgiving terrain, securing critical infrastructure, or simply making an unforgettable statement at your next corporate picnic. From the rugged reliability of our "Plowshare" series (perfect for extreme land clearing) to the bespoke luxury of our "Executive Enforcer" line (featuring custom interiors and state-of-the-art defensive systems), every vehicle in our inventory is rigorously inspected, meticulously maintained, and ready to roll.
